Guided Tour in the City of Tirana

Explore the bustling capital of Albania with a professional guide. Dive into culture, history, and local flavors.

Tour Overview

The capital of Albania, Tirana, is a bustling city founded in 1614 by Sulejman Pashe Bargjini. It combines culture, history, contemporary architecture, and a warm hospitality shown to tourists.

As you take a walk in Tirana, you will enjoy colorful buildings, pleasant caf√©s, and green parks. The tour includes walking along the main boulevard, visiting Skanderbeg Square, Mother Teresa Square, and other cultural sites. We will end the tour with lunch at a local restaurant featuring traditional and modern Albanian dishes. The experience lasts about 3 hours, including lunch.

Sites We Will Visit

  • Skanderbeg Square - the main square of Tirana
  • The Ethem Bey Mosque
  • The Resurrection of Christ Orthodox Church
  • National Historical Museum (exterior only, due to restoration work)
  • The Palace of Culture and National Library
  • The Clock Tower
  • The House of Leaves (former espionage building during the communist era)
  • The Villa of Enver Hoxha (former dictator of Albania)

What's Included

  • Professional tour guide for the duration of the tour
  • Excursion as per the described itinerary
  • Lunch at a local restaurant in Tirana

What's Excluded

  • Entrance fees to museums and monuments (optional)
  • Drinks and beverages during lunch
